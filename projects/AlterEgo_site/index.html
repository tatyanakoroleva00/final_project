Hi, my name is Annabelle!

Since early childhood, I've been interested in languages. Languages is the whole world for me, the whole Universe.

This is the website where I'd love to share with you all my creativity.

I enjoy studying languages, no matter human or computer ones. The joy I get is precise! They help me to understand people and books, travel the world and get acquainted with new cultures. I am fond of writing and performing on stage with the verses. I also have a YouTube channel where I teach my francophone viewers Russian language. I love taking pictures of places that I visit and people that I see because every moment is precious and I opened to adventures!
